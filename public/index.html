Wild Beasts Fest


Get ready to unleash your inner beast at the wildest music festival of the year! Beast Fest is not your average music festival. It's an unforgettable experience that combines the best of music, art, and entertainment to create a one-of-a-kind event that you won't want to miss.
Join us for a weekend of non-stop fun as we bring together a lineup of the most hilarious artists and performers from around the world. From comedy to music, to interactive performances, there's something for everyone at Beast Fest.

You'll be immersed in a world of color and creativity as we transform our festival grounds into a magical wonderland of vibrant art installations, interactive exhibits, and mind-bending performances.
But that's not all! We've got plenty of surprises in store for you, including food trucks serving up delicious bites, craft vendors selling unique handmade goods, and an array of fun-filled activities that will keep you entertained from dawn till dusk. So grab your wildest outfit, bring your friends, and get ready to let loose and have the time of your life at Beast Fest!
